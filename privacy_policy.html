StuPlayer Privacy Policy and Contact Information



1. Data access, data collection, data use, and data sharing

No data, personal or otherwise, is collected by the StuPlayer app.



2. MacOS permissions


StuPlayer asks the user to grant access to the folder containing m3u files and tracks.

The app calls this folder the root folder. Read and write access to the root folder is required.


However, write access is only used by the app to produce log files.

The log files are called StuPlay0.log and StuPlayer1.log and they are written to the root folder.



3. Advertising

StuPlayer does not contain ads.



4. Contact information

Enquires about the app and its Privacy Policy can be addressed to stuplayer@fig7.com

Please also send bug reports to stuplayer@fig7.com
